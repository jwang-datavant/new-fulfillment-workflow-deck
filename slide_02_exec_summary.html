Sequential ROI Fulfillment Is Holding Us Back — Here Is Our Hypothesis to Fix It
A complex patient request spans multiple systems and physical locations. Today, one operations staff member handles all tasks sequentially — context switching, traveling, and waiting. We believe splitting requests into parallel task units will fundamentally improve how fulfillment works.
